About The Challenge

With the Saving Semakau Challenge, we aim to take the main objective of a hackathon, a platform of ideation, and use that as a way to come up with solutions to more specific problems, like the ones in our community. We will also tap on different resources from the community or industry in order to support any ideas that have potential, to hopefully implement them into the community.

It will be run by various student interest groups across polytechnics and be held in one of the polytechnics. The competition will consist of webinars and workshops, as well as regular consultations with industry experts and grassroot leaders.

We are building the foundations to change the attitude of the community towards sustainability. With this event, we can reach a big audience and expose them to the issues facing Semakau landfill and educate them about the dire consequences. We are also bringing the community and industry closer together, which will hopefully lead to fruitful discussions and cooperation between both parties.
